Elsa Leo-Rhynie Hall




Welcome to the Elsa Leo-Rhynie Hall! The place where you “Lead the pack on the hunt for excellence.”

On behalf of the residential and commuting students, alumni, friends and family of The University of the West Indies, Mona Campus community it is my pleasure to welcome you to Elsa Leo-Rhynie Hall.


Elsa Leo-Rhynie Hall (Towers) opened its doors September 1, 2012 to 132 students. Another 132 students joined the Hall on September 8, 2012 when another tower was opened. All five towers were opened in August 2013 and we now offer accommodation to 600 students each academic year.

There are five towers (Tower of Dragons, Tower of Orion, Tower of Apollo, Tower of Phoenix and Tower of Olympus ) four of which are single occupancy, the other being double occupancy. All the single occupancy towers have a capacity of 132 spaces.

The double occupancy tower has four levels. Level one is used as the Administrative Floor with the Manager’s Office, Study Rooms, Barbershop, Commissary and Café, Athenaeum, Commuting Student Lounge, Conference Room and a fully equipped Computer Room. Levels two and three have a total of 24 rooms and houses 48 students. Level four has 12 rooms and is used to house the Resident Advisors.
